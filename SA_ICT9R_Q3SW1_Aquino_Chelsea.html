Netflix TV Series: Lucifer


Summary & Characters:

A popular Netflix Series, Lucifer. The King of Hell got bored being the headmaster of Hell, so he decided to take a vacation to Earth in the city of Angels, Los Angeles. He stays in Earth for years, he owns a club named Lux. His dear friend was murdered by a person and he was pulled in for questioning because he was a suspect, then there he meets Detective Chloe Decker. Throughout the series they both work together as partners.

Lucifer Morningstar

Just by reading the name, you already know who this is. The Devil, Satan, King of Hell. Lucifer is a charming person, he can be selfish and greedy most of the time but not all the time. Despite him being the Devil himself, he can be caring.

Chloe Decker

Chloe Decker is a good detective for the LAPD, she is the partner of Lucifer. She's a single mother, she used to be married to another detective, Dan Espinoza but they got divorced. Chloe gets irritated by Lucifer but Chloe also loves Lucifer.

Mazikeen Smith

Mazikeen or Maze is a Demon, the best friend of Lucifer. She helps Lucifer with anything so she is always by his side. She joined Lucifer when he decided to take a vacation to LA. Maze also became a bounty hunter for the LAPD.

Amenadiel

One of Lucifer's brother, Amenadiel the eldest angel. God sent him to try to bring Lucifer back to hell. He failed many times and was pursuaded by Lucifer to see why he loves Earth. Amenadiel then sees why and decided to stay, years later he met a woman named Linda and had a baby angel with her.

Linda Martin

Dr. Martin is Lucifer's therapist, she tries to help Lucifer with all of his problems and even when Lucifer gets the wrong idea Linda would still try to help him because not only is she a good doctor she is also a good friend to Lucifer. Linda is also the former lover of Amenadiel, she and Amenadiel have a baby angel named Charlie.

Ella Lopez

Ella Lopez works in the LAPD with Chloe and Lucifer. She's a forensic scientist, she helps in gathering evidence and helps in finding the murderers. Ella is a sweet and outgoing person who always try to make everyone happy with a famous Ella hug.

Dan Espinoza

Detective Espinoza also works in the LAPD. He is also the ex-husband of Chloe Decker, they both have a daughter together name Trixie. Dan is Lucifer's favorite person to annoy because of the reaction he gets as a result. Even if Lucifer and Dan don't like each other very much, they can work together very well.

Trixie Espinoza

Trixie is the only daughter of Chloe Decker and Dan Espinoza. She has been in the TV Series since the first season, when Lucifer introduced him as the Devil Trixie did not seem fazed by it. She loves Lucifer no matter what.

Aurora (Rory) Decker Morningstar

Lastly we have Rory the daughter of Chloe Decker and Lucifer Morningstar. She appeared in the final season of Lucifer. Rory is a unique angel because her wings are red and very sharp, sharp enough to nick a celestial being. Rory traveled from the future to the present time to "kill" her father but decided not to. She hates Lucifer because she thought that he abandonded her and Chloe.
